Technical details

The Earth Kurma group uses a variety of custom malware and tools, including rootkits like KRNRAT and MORIYA, to maintain a foothold in compromised networks. They utilize cloud services for data exfiltration and employ various techniques for lateral movement and credential theft.

Recommendations

  • Enforce strict driver installation policies.
  • Strengthen Active Directory and DFSR controls.
  • Limit SMB communications across the network.

Hunting & detection
